Biotech Strategy · Regulatory · Case Study
Iovance Biotherapeutics — Amtagvi
A decision-oriented case study examining the strategic, financial, and organizational challenges facing Iovance after FDA approval of Amtagvi — the first tumor-infiltrating lymphocyte therapy for advanced melanoma. Explores how a company should allocate limited resources across commercialization, manufacturing, and pipeline development when the science works but the systems around it are under pressure. Co-authored with Prof. George Whaley, presented at MBAA 2026.
Presented Ask me →
Supply Chain · Operations · Market Modeling
Supply Chain Design for a Biologics Company
Designed a full end-to-end supply chain for a monoclonal antibody therapy entering Phase III — from raw materials to patient delivery across seven stages. Led the market modeling and demand forecasting, building three scenarios (conservative, base, optimistic) based on different penetration assumptions. Bioreactor capacity at the API manufacturing stage turned out to be the primary bottleneck — counterintuitive, and exactly the kind of thing that matters.
